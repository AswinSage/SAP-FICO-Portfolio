# Scenarios

## Purpose

This folder contains practical scenarios demonstrating how SAP S/4HANA Enterprise Structure is designed to meet different business requirements.

## Planned Scenarios

- Manufacturing Enterprise
- Retail Organization
- Logistics Company
- Service Organization
- Multi-Country Enterprise
- Shared Service Center

## Objectives

- Understand business requirements.
- Design an appropriate Enterprise Structure.
- Relate SAP organizational units to real-world organizations.
- Analyze implementation decisions.
